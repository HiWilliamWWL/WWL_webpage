About Me

Hello! I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura . Presently, I am also a visiting researcher at the GRASP lab, University of Pennsylvania, collaborating with Prof. Lingjie Liu and Prof. Dinesh Jayaraman. I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science from the Paul G. Allen School at the University of Washington. My primary research domains include motion synthesis, 3D human reconstruction, and robotics.
